PROFILE

Hello everyone! My name is Karlina Martin! I am a 28-year-old student, born locally in Albuquerque, New Mexico. I am majoring in Psychology, with a minor in Communications. I am on track to graduate in the summer of 2024! I am a returning student. I completed my associate degree at CNM when I was 22 years old. Since then, I have been working at different schools around Albuquerque. I started at a preschool. After that, I began teaching music at a private school. Now, I am teaching in the intellectually disabled program at an elementary school. I have worked with little kids, up to seniors in high school. Surprisingly, one of my favorite demographics to work with has been the parents of my students. Both Psychology and Communications have been excellent tools in my work life. I have used the knowledge from these fields to elevate my teachings.

HOBBY

FORMAT
